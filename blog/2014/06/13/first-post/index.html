First Post!

2014-06-13 :: Meta, Personal

Huzzah! Wewewew! Will you look at that, first post.

Hi everyone, I’m Matias Eyzaguirre and this is my blog of stuff. Here you will find lots of cool things and projects of mine, in addition to ramblings about life, the universe, and everything.

As I said, I am Matias Eyzaguirre. I am a 23 year old gay male living in Utah. I like math, programming, science fiction, crochet, and tatting among other things. A problem with trying to describe myself is that it’s hard to prevent it from sounding like a personals ad, so I think I’ll stop here.

A blog seems like the sort of thing that someone like me would have, and I’ve been thinking of starting one for a while now. Thinking about it won’t do much by itself, so It’s time to stop putting it off and just do it.

I’ll be honest, I’m still not really sure what kind of material will go into this blog. It’ll probably end up consisting of posts about math or programming, along with little programming how-tos for various tasks that I find myself needing to do without any documentation to rely on. And of course I’ll also do write-ups of any mini-projects that I think are interesting, and possible progress reports for larger projects, who knows?
